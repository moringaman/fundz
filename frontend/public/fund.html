Our Approach

Markets are complex.
So is our team.

Capital flows from our Portfolio Manager to competing Trader AIs, each backed by a different frontier language model. Traders that perform earn more capital. Traders that underperform are reallocated. The fund evolves — continuously.

How It Works

Every 5 minutes. Relentlessly.

The fund's decision engine runs a complete analysis and reallocation cycle every 5 minutes, with deeper strategic reviews every 20 minutes.

STEP 01
Market Analysis

Research Analyst determines market regime: trending, ranging, or volatile across all active pairs

STEP 02
TA Confluence

Technical Analyst scores each symbol: RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, ATR, pattern count

STEP 03
PM Allocates to Traders

James allocates 15–50% to each trader based on performance, market fit, and strategy type

STEP 04
Trader Sub-Allocation

Each trader distributes its budget across its own strategies using win-rate weighting

STEP 05
Risk Gate

Risk Manager checks daily P&L, exposure, and concentration. Blocks all execution if danger level

STEP 06
Signal + Execute

Strategies generate buy/sell signals. Trade gates verify TP covers fees + 0.5% minimum net profit

STEP 07
Learn & Adapt

Closed trades feed the Retrospective AI. SL/TP parameters updated per strategy. Metrics persisted to DB
